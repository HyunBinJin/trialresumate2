Frequently Asked Questions

What is Resumate?

Resumate is a platform that helps you create a professional resume by simply filling out your details. It allows you to input personal information, education, experience, and more.

How do I create a resume?

To create a resume, click on the 'Info Input' page and fill out the form with your details. Once you're done, go to the 'Preview & Edit' page to review and download your resume.

Can I edit my resume later?

Yes! You can go back to the 'Info Input' page anytime to update your information. Once you've made changes, you can see the updated resume on the 'Preview & Edit' page.

Is there any cost involved?

No, Resumate is free to use! You can create and download your resume without any charges.

What file formats can I download my resume in?

Currently, Resumate allows you to download your resume as a PDF file. We plan to add more formats in the future.
